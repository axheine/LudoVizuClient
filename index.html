Hello and welcome to the LudoVizu platform. This website is designed to make an inventory of Ile St-Louis old plans and to share a visualisation along the time. Follow the path of this island History across the maps and discover how it evolved at the same time as Paris. Click on a date to begin your journey.

The georeferencing of “Ile Saint-Louis” involves providing a spatial location to images we have previously selected. Theses raster files (pdf,jpeg or png) are representative of the island during several era of Paris History.
